Introduction

Welcome to the online voter enrolment portal. This platform allows eligible citizens to register as voters conveniently and efficiently. Our goal is to streamline the registration process and ensure that every eligible voter can participate in the democratic process.

Rules and Regulations

  • Applicants must be citizens of the country.
  • Applicants must be at least 18 years old.
  • Only one registration per person is allowed.
  • Provide accurate and up-to-date information.
  • Verify the provided information before submission.
